About Us

Who We Are

Rocky TechSavvy is a community-focused digital training centre. Founded by Peter Moenga, we empower youth and job-seekers with practical computer skills and confidence to access digital opportunities.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide by providing affordable, practical training and access to technology for youth and communities in Kisii County.

Services

Computer Training

Beginner to intermediate courses on MS Office, programming basics, and digital literacy tailored for all learners.

Digital Literacy Workshops

Short practical workshops for schools, youth groups, and community organizations focused on online safety, email, and job search skills.

Internet & Printing

Reliable internet, printing, scanning, and CV/letter typing services to support students and professionals.

CV Design & Job Readiness

One-on-one CV assistance, interview preparation, and guidance on applying for jobs and internships.
